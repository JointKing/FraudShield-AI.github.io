Protecting Consumers with Advanced Fraud Prevention

FraudShield AI offers cutting-edge protection against fraudulent transactions, ensuring your financial safety. Our AI-driven solutions provide real-time detection and prevention, reducing the risk of unauthorized charges and enhancing your online shopping experience.
  • Disposable Digital Cards – Prevents fraud by generating temporary, one-time-use card numbers.
  • AI-Powered Fraud Detection – Monitors transactions in real time to detect and block suspicious activity.
  • Single-Use Cards – Automatically cancels after one purchase, eliminating the risk of data breaches.
  • Merchant-Specific Cards – Limits virtual cards to a single merchant, preventing unauthorized use elsewhere.
  • Customizable Spending Limits – Set daily, weekly, or per-transaction limits to prevent overcharges.
  • Free Trial Virtual Cards – Creates a card that automatically cancels within 24 hours, avoiding hidden charges.
  • Subscription Control – Consumers can cancel virtual cards linked to subscriptions instead of contacting merchants.
